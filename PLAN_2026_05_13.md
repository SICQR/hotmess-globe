---

## The frame

Three things are true at once.

1. **The v6 Ghosted loop is structurally complete behind a flag.** ProfileMediaStack physics, IntentLayer reveal, MutualStateOverlay, RecoveryState, soft logistics. All wired. The audit confirms the gating, telemetry, and DB shape are sound. Real-device calibration is the only thing standing between this and a wider ramp.

2. **The platform around it is leaking.** The audit surfaced 8 P0s that silently break user flows: `profile_views` RLS-on-no-policies kills view tracking; `events` RLS allows creation but not reading; `shop-cart` and `listing` sheets aren't even registered; L2ChatSheet still writes to deprecated tables that drop in 8 days. None of these are visible to me from looking at the v6 loop. All of them are visible to a user trying to do basic things.

3. **The brand is drifting.** 13 files using `amber-400` instead of `#C8962C`. Four radii in one viewport. Two card languages. Forty files violating the weights-400/500 rule. Ghosted is the only surface that's been disciplined. The rest of the app is still wearing last quarter's clothes.

**The plan resolves these in the order they bite users.** Stop-the-bleed first, brand cohesion second, deep migrations third, calibration and ramp last.

## Hard calls I'm making (cofounder authority)

These are decisions I'm acting on without further consultation. Push back now if any are wrong.

1. **Retire `L2GhostedPreviewSheet` entirely.** Task #39 has been in-progress for weeks. The audit confirms it's "static / unusable." We just routed `/ghosted` taps to `L2ProfileSheet` instead. Killing it removes a dead surface and a confusing registry entry. The full profile sheet IS the preview — it loads fast enough.

2. **Card surface canonical: `#1C1C1E` token wins.** Market, Music, More, content cards, and sheets all use it. Home/Pulse/Ghosted use translucent white (`rgba(255,255,255,0.03–0.05)`) which breaks consistency on dark backgrounds and reads as "not finished." Migrate the three offenders to the token.

3. **Radius canonical:** `--hm-radius-card: 16px` (rounded-2xl), `--hm-radius-control: 12px` (rounded-xl), `--hm-radius-pill: 2px` (Ghosted filter chips), `--hm-radius-fab: 9999px`. Codify in `index.css`. Everything else gets rewritten to these tokens. `rounded-3xl` (24px) on hero CTAs goes away.

4. **Gold canonical:** `#C8962C`. Tailwind `amber-*` classes are banned from the codebase. CSS variable `--hm-gold` is the new way to reference it; rgba variants accepted for alpha overlays. Pre-commit lint rule blocks `amber-` going forward.

5. **Weights canonical:** 400 (regular), 500 (bold). The Ghosted home + card patterns are the reference. Everything `font-bold` / `font-black` / `font-semibold` outside Ghosted gets rebased to 500 unless it's a price (700 allowed because money is the exception across every consumer product).

6. **Bottom nav: 5 tabs is the truth.** More is reached via long-press on the rightmost tab or by URL. CLAUDE.md gets updated. No 6th tab.

7. **/care and /radio: redirects to /more are the truth.** Update CLAUDE.md. Stop documenting them as live routes.

8. **/profile destination: `ProfileMode` not `/settings`.** Currently `MorePage > My Profile` bounces to settings. Fix the route to land on the actual profile view.

9. **Stuck-flag triage.** Any flag that's Phil-only with no signoff note AND older than 60 days gets either a one-line note ("ship by X" or "abandoned") or it's deleted. I'll prepare the list; you sign off.

10. **Defer events seeding (#54–58) until after Week 2.** The audit shows `events` table has 0 rows — there's nothing to migrate. The work is greenfield. We seed AFTER the platform stops leaking, not while it's leaking.
