iOS PUSH = INSTALLED PWA ONLY
You're in regular Safari on iPhone. Apple does not expose Notification or PushManager APIs to Safari outside an installed home-screen PWA. No code can change this.

To enable push on this iPhone:
1. Tap the Share button (square with arrow up) in the Safari toolbar
2. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Confirm "Add"
4. Open HOTMESS from your home screen icon
5. Inside the PWA, go to Settings → Push Notifications and flip the toggle

Once installed, this debug page also works from inside the PWA — open it via Settings → Push Notifications → "open push debug".
