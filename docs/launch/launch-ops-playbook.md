# Launch Ops Playbook

## Purpose

Define how HOTMESS launches districts safely while preserving:
- truth
- readability
- trust
- moderation stability

Launch quality matters more than growth speed.

---

# Launch Priorities

1. Safety
2. Truth
3. Trust
4. Readability
5. Density Stability
6. Monetization

---

# District Rollout Rules

Never open a district without:
- verified venue coverage
- moderation readiness
- trust seeding
- saturation monitoring
- stale leakage monitoring

---

# Venue Seeding

Initial districts require:
- anchor venues
- trusted operators
- reliable operational updates
- early moderation relationships

Venue truth stabilizes the map.

---

# Promoter Controls

At launch:
- promoter boosts remain tightly capped
- repeated amplification limited aggressively
- district dominance prevented early

No promoter should define district reality.

---

# Density Orchestration

During launch:
- cluster aggressively
- collapse duplicates early
- preserve readability over completeness
- prioritize trusted local truth

Low density is acceptable.

Synthetic density is forbidden.

---

# Quiet-State Handling

Quiet districts should:
- surface verified venues
- show upcoming events
- preserve truthful emptiness

Never simulate urgency.

---

# Moderation Readiness

Before expansion:
- moderation SLAs must be healthy
- escalation paths must exist
- abuse review capacity must be sufficient

Unmoderated growth is system damage.

---

# Trust Seeding

Early launch trust should rely on:
- verified venues
- reliable promoters
- moderation-reviewed contributors
- operational consistency

Trust inflation should remain slow.

---

# Saturation Controls

Monitor:
- paid visibility share
- source concentration
- repeated boosts
- clutter score
- district congestion

Clamp aggressively when saturation rises.

---

# Launch Readiness Gates

Districts are ready only when:
- stale leakage remains low
- moderation keeps pace
- trust mix stabilizes
- venue coverage reaches threshold
- readability remains strong

---

# Rollback Rules

Slow or pause rollout when:
- moderation falls behind
- false urgency rises
- trust collapses
- districts become unreadable
- spam concentration spikes

Integrity outranks growth velocity.
