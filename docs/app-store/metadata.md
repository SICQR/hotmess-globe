```
HOTMESS is London's queer nightlife operating system — built for gay men who want to live safely, connect authentically, and experience the city fully.

No swiping. No ghosts. No algorithm deciding who you see.

━━━ RIGHT NOW ━━━
Drop your presence on the live globe. See who's out tonight, where the energy is, and what's happening near you — in real time. Right Now signals expire automatically. No permanent history.

━━━ THE GLOBE ━━━
A 3D live map of London's queer night. Beacons, events, and real-time signals pulse across the city. See the night come alive before you leave the house.

━━━ MEET ━━━
Find someone tonight. HOTMESS shows who's nearby and out right now. Walking ETAs. A guided meet flow with mutual confirmation — both of you confirm before anything moves. Care built in.

━━━ SAFETY OS ━━━
Built for nights that don't always go to plan.

• SOS button — sends your location to trusted contacts instantly
• Safe journey sharing — share your ETA, get confirmation you arrived
• Backup contacts — designate who gets called if you don't check in
• Land in Safety — a quiet space to decompress, on your terms
• Check-in system — let someone know you're okay

━━━ RADIO ━━━
HOTMESS Radio is live, 24/7. DJ sets, curated shows, and the sound of the night — streaming direct from our studio. Your soundtrack from pre-game to aftercare.

━━━ MARKET ━━━
Shop the HOTMESS edit. Merch, preloved gear, and community-first commerce. Powered by Shopify. No algorithm, no recommendation engine — just things worth having.

━━━ WINGMAN + SCENE SCOUT ━━━
Your AI co-pilot for the night. Scene Scout finds the right venue based on what's happening. Wingman writes an icebreaker when you need one. All optional. All private. Disable in Settings at any time.

━━━ CONSENT-FIRST ━━━
Ask first. Confirm yes. Respect no. No pressure.

Every HOTMESS feature is designed around this principle. Our meet flow requires mutual confirmation. Our presence model is opt-in. Our safety tools are there when you need them.

18+ · Consent-first · Care always.
```
