# HOTMESS — brand voice

One-pager voice spec for HOTMESS copy. Codified from Sprint 0 closeout v3 corrective. All member-facing copy (email, web, in-app text) should pass this filter.

## Register

Queer-direct, sex-positive, recovery-informed, no apology for what we are, no corporate softening. Terse. Confident. Founder-personal where relevant.

## Sentence shape

Short. Statements not pitches. Fragments OK where they hit. No hyperbole, no marketing adjectives ("amazing", "incredible", "unique"), no exclamation points outside Phil's signature line.

## What we never sound like

Grindr/Sniffies in horny mode. Corporate queer-washing. Recovery-pamphlet-soft. Startup-pitch.

## What we always sound like

Phil at 7am on his porch in Samui telling you what he built and why. The auth screen on `hotmessldn.com` is the visual reference — monospace tech aesthetic, dark, terse buttons, no chrome.

## Words we use

`queer`, `the globe`, `the platform`, `we`, `you`, `real`, `works`, `proximity`, `care`, `recovery`, `sobriety as identity`, `OG`, `you turned up`, `thank you`, `I owed you`.

## Words we avoid

`exclusive`, `limited time`, `don't miss out`, `incredible`, `cohort opens`, `scarcity` framing, anything that smells of a marketing funnel.

## Numbers and prices

Stated plainly, no currency-symbol drama. `£7.99/mo`, not `JUST £7.99/MO!`. Inline with sentence flow when possible.

## Compliance bottom-edge

Unsubscribe + physical address as required by law/Resend deliverability. Single line. No icons, no socials, no "view in browser".
