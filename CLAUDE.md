## The Canonical Decision Hierarchy

**Immutable. Inherited by every downstream system.**

1. **Safety**
2. **Truth**
3. **Trust**
4. **Freshness**
5. **Momentum**
6. **Readability**
7. **Relevance**
8. **Monetization**

> **Never reorder. Never abbreviate. Never collapse.**

If two governance docs disagree on this list, [`docs/doctrine/sacred-invariants.md`](docs/doctrine/sacred-invariants.md) wins.

## Compliance — non-negotiables

- **No synthetic urgency.**
- **No fake density.**
- **No exact user tracking by default.**
- **No monetization override of trust or safety.**
- **All loops reversible and auditable.**
- **Moderation actions logged.**
- **Trust decay mandatory.**
- **Visibility states explicit.**

## Doctrine in one line

> **HOTMESS is a realtime trust-weighted civic visibility system. The system fails the moment synthetic visibility becomes easier than truthful visibility.**
